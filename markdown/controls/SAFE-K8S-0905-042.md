# SAFE-K8S-0905-042 - CTA-2114 ML-BOM generation and lineage metadata capture

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must generate ML Bills of Materials for production-bound models that capture model provenance metadata including training data lineage, hyperparameters, framework versions, and training infrastructure identity.

## Objective

Maintain auditable model lineage transparency by ensuring production-bound models have structured ML-BOM records with the metadata needed to trace their origin and training conditions.

## Rationale

Without ML-BOM generation and metadata capture, organizations cannot reliably trace which datasets, framework versions, and training conditions produced a model. That weakens impact analysis for vulnerability disclosures, provenance investigations, and data correction or deletion events.

## Scope

Applies to production-bound model artifacts generated or promoted through Kubernetes-connected AI delivery workflows. Covers CTA-2114-aligned ML-BOM generation and capture of model lineage metadata. Excludes durable attachment and storage mechanics for those records.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: ML-BOM generation tooling integrated into training and registration workflows.
- Implementation Surface: CTA-2114-aligned ML-BOM tooling, capture of model identity, training data lineage, hyperparameters, framework versions, and training infrastructure metadata.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; DevOps Engineering
