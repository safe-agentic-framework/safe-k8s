# SAFE-K8S-0905-022 - ML artifact signing and training attestation generation

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific
- Superseded By: SAFE-K8S-0905-046

## Statement

All ML lifecycle artifacts produced by internal training and build pipelines must be cryptographically signed using Sigstore or equivalent signing infrastructure, and in-toto or equivalent attestations must be generated for the training pipeline steps that produced those artifacts.

## Objective

Establish build-time cryptographic chain of custody for model artifacts by signing internally produced artifacts and generating attestations that prove how they were built.

## Rationale

Without cryptographic signing and verifiable training attestations, there is no trustworthy proof that a model was produced by an authorized pipeline or that the build steps and inputs were not tampered with. Build-time signing and attestation generation create the evidence that downstream verification and promotion controls rely on.

## Scope

Applies to models, training input manifests, intermediate pipeline artifacts, final outputs, and related provenance records generated by internal ML training and packaging pipelines. Covers signing and attestation generation, but not deploy-time verification or registry retention policies.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Model signing pipeline and training attestation generation workflow.
- Implementation Surface: Sigstore cosign or equivalent signing infrastructure with identity-based trust, keyless or managed-key signing for models and related artifacts, in-toto attestation generation for training pipeline steps, attestation storage alongside OCI model artifacts, and internal re-signing workflow for externally sourced models admitted into internal registries.
- Owner Primary: ML Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; DevOps Engineering
