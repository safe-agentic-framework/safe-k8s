# SAFE-K8S-1001-022 - Regulatory audit log retention period enforcement

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations subject to regulatory requirements must configure audit log storage systems with retention period settings that meet or exceed the minimum periods mandated by applicable regulations. Retention period enforcement must use immutable or WORM-compliant storage controls that prevent ordinary deletion before the configured period expires, and automated compliance checks must detect under-configured retention periods.

## Objective

Ensure that regulatory audit logs for Kubernetes AI workloads are retained for the full period required by applicable regulations with automated enforcement that prevents premature deletion.

## Rationale

Regulatory frameworks impose minimum retention periods for audit logs related to AI system operation. If retention periods are under-configured or can be bypassed through ordinary operations, organizations lose evidence needed for compliance audits, incident investigations, and post-market monitoring. This control isolates period configuration and deletion prevention from the separate obligation to keep retained logs durably retrievable.

## Scope

Applies to audit log storage systems receiving Kubernetes and AI workload audit data subject to regulatory retention requirements. Covers retention period configuration, immutability settings, and automated compliance verification. Excludes durable retrieval testing and format accessibility, which are addressed separately.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Audit Log Storage Retention Policy Enforcement
- Implementation Surface: Object storage retention lock configuration, log management system retention settings, SIEM retention policy configuration, immutable storage controls, and automated retention compliance scanning.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
