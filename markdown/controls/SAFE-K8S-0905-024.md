# SAFE-K8S-0905-024 - Authenticated reviewer identity validation and approval audit binding

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Model promotion approval workflows must validate reviewer identity through authenticated approval mechanisms and must record reviewer identity in the promotion audit trail.

## Objective

Ensure that model promotion approvals are attributable to authenticated and authorized reviewers with auditable identity binding.

## Rationale

Without reviewer identity validation and audit binding, approval actions can be forged, replayed, or attributed to unauthorized personnel, undermining the trustworthiness of human promotion decisions for production models.

## Scope

Applies to all model promotion workflows requiring human approval on Kubernetes. Covers reviewer authentication, approval identity capture, and audit trail linkage between the reviewer and the promoted model version. Excludes separation-of-duties enforcement, which is covered by SAFE-K8S-0905-025.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Approval workflow identity provider integration and audit trail capture point.
- Implementation Surface: Promotion approval workflows integrated with SSO or OIDC, authenticated approval actions, reviewer identity capture, and audit records linking approver identity, timestamp, and approval decision to the promoted model version.
- Owner Primary: Governance
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
