# SAFE-K8S-0404-005 - Certificate rotation execution and expiry monitoring

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.4 - Certificate Management
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must automate certificate rotation before expiry and implement monitoring and alerting for certificates approaching defined expiration thresholds across cluster and workload PKI assets.

## Objective

Prevent certificate-expiry outages by ensuring certificate renewal occurs before expiration and by providing actionable visibility into certificates nearing expiry.

## Rationale

Expired certificates immediately disrupt TLS handshakes and often trigger emergency workarounds that weaken security. Automated renewal combined with expiry monitoring reduces outage risk and gives operators time to correct renewal failures before service impact.

## Scope

Applies to API server, etcd peer, kubelet, webhook, service mesh, and application certificates managed in the cluster. Covers rotation configuration, expiry thresholds, dashboards, and alerts.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Certificate renewal configuration and expiry telemetry for cluster PKI assets.
- Implementation Surface: cert-manager Certificate resources with renewBefore settings, renewal automation, expiry dashboards, Prometheus or scanner-based certificate age telemetry, and alerting integrations for near-expiry certificates.
- Owner Primary: Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; Security Operations
