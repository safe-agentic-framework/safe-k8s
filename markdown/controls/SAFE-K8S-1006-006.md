# SAFE-K8S-1006-006 - Continuous compliance evidence generation for AI workloads

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.6 - Compliance and Governance
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations must continuously generate, retain, and export compliance evidence from policy-as-code enforcement decisions, including policy reports, admission allow and deny events, policy version history, and documented policy exceptions with approval and expiration metadata.

## Objective

Produce audit-ready compliance evidence continuously so control effectiveness is demonstrable without manual reconstruction.

## Rationale

Without structured evidence generation, compliance posture is difficult to prove and gaps are detected late during audits. Continuous evidence collection improves audit readiness and governance assurance.

## Scope

Applies to policy-enforced AI workloads and associated governance reporting pipelines. Covers evidence capture, retention, and export for audit and compliance functions.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Policy reporting pipelines, admission decision logs, and evidence export integrations.
- Implementation Surface: PolicyReport and Gatekeeper audit outputs, SIEM exports, GRC evidence repositories, exception approval workflows, and policy source control history.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; AI/ML Engineering; Security Engineering
