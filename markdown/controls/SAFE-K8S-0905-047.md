# SAFE-K8S-0905-047 - Training pipeline attestation generation for ML artifacts

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

In-toto or equivalent attestations must be generated for the training and build pipeline steps that produced internal ML artifacts so the provenance of those artifacts can be independently evaluated.

## Objective

Establish verifiable provenance for internally produced ML artifacts by generating attestations that record how the artifacts were built.

## Rationale

Cryptographic signatures prove artifact integrity, but not how the artifact was produced. Training pipeline attestations provide verifiable build-step provenance, inputs, and workflow evidence that downstream trust decisions depend on.

## Scope

Applies to models, training input manifests, intermediate pipeline artifacts, final outputs, and related provenance records generated by internal ML training and packaging pipelines. Covers attestation generation for training and build steps, but not deploy-time verification or registry retention policies.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Training attestation generation workflow.
- Implementation Surface: In-toto attestation generation for training pipeline steps, provenance predicate capture, attestation subjects for models and related artifacts, and attestation storage alongside OCI model artifacts.
- Owner Primary: ML Platform Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Security Engineering; DevOps Engineering
