# SAFE-K8S-0908-007 - Differential privacy parameter governance and budget tracking for inference

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.8 - Model Abuse and Extraction Prevention
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Inference endpoints serving models trained on sensitive data must configure differential privacy parameters with documented privacy guarantees and must track cumulative privacy budget consumption per model or principal so privacy-loss thresholds are enforced before responses are served.

## Objective

Mathematically bound inference-time information leakage by governing differential privacy parameters and enforcing privacy budget limits over repeated access.

## Rationale

Differential privacy is only defensible when the privacy parameters and cumulative budget consumption are explicitly managed. Without parameter governance and budget tracking, repeated queries can exhaust the intended privacy guarantee even if noise is added to individual responses.

## Scope

Applies to inference endpoints on Kubernetes serving models trained on sensitive data where formal privacy guarantees are required. Covers epsilon and delta configuration, privacy budget accounting, and privacy guarantee validation. Excludes output perturbation mechanics for non-formal privacy controls and secure aggregation for aggregate queries.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Differential privacy parameter configuration and privacy budget tracking middleware.
- Implementation Surface: Differential privacy parameter settings per model, privacy budget accounting middleware, per-principal or per-model cumulative spend monitoring, threshold enforcement logic, and privacy audit validation of formal guarantees.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Privacy Engineering
