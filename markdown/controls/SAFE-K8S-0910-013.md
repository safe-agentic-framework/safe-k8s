# SAFE-K8S-0910-013 - Unified multi-cluster compliance reporting and governance coordination

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.10 - Multi-Cluster and Federated AI Security
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations operating AI workloads across multiple Kubernetes clusters must maintain unified compliance reporting that spans cluster boundaries and must coordinate centralized secret, certificate, and incident governance across the federation.

## Objective

Provide unified governance visibility and coordinated cross-cluster security operations for multi-cluster AI platforms.

## Rationale

Even if policy is distributed consistently, governance fails when compliance evidence is fragmented, secrets and certificates are managed inconsistently across clusters, or incident response is handled cluster by cluster without federation-wide coordination. Unified reporting and coordinated governance operations are required for holistic assurance.

## Scope

Applies to organizations operating AI workloads across multiple Kubernetes clusters. Covers unified compliance reporting, centralized secret and certificate governance, and multi-cluster incident coordination. Excludes policy federation and drift detection, which are covered by SAFE-K8S-0910-012.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Unified reporting platform and coordinated governance operations process.
- Implementation Surface: Compliance evidence aggregation across clusters, centralized secret management with per-cluster authentication, centralized certificate lifecycle governance, and multi-cluster incident response coordination procedures and exercises.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; GRC/Compliance; ML Platform Engineering
