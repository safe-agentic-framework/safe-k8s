# SAFE-K8S-1001-018 - Regulatory audit log retention period and durable retrieval enforcement

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations subject to regulatory requirements must enforce retention periods and durable retrieval capabilities for audit logs covering Kubernetes and AI workload events, ensuring that audit records remain available for the mandated period and cannot be prematurely deleted through ordinary operations.

## Objective

Ensure regulatory-required audit logs remain retained and retrievable for the full compliance period.

## Rationale

Compliance investigations and post-incident reviews depend on preserved audit logs. If audit data ages out too early or cannot be retrieved when needed, organizations lose the evidence required to demonstrate compliance and investigate AI-system events.

## Scope

Applies to audit logs for Kubernetes control plane events, AI workload operations, and other regulatory-relevant security logs retained in centralized logging or archival platforms. Excludes retention of model artifacts and provenance records.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Log management retention configuration and long-term retrieval controls.
- Implementation Surface: Centralized logging retention rules, long-term log archive configuration, WORM or immutable retention options for critical audit records, and validation that retained logs remain searchable or retrievable throughout the mandated period.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
