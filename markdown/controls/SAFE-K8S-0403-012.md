# SAFE-K8S-0403-012 - Automated credential inventory and orphaned credential reconciliation

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.3 - Secrets Management
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations running AI workloads at scale must maintain an automated credential inventory tracking all active secrets, tokens, and keys issued to AI workloads, and must detect and remediate orphaned credentials left behind by completed or failed jobs.

## Objective

Provide visibility into the active credential surface of AI workloads and ensure abandoned credentials are reconciled and removed promptly.

## Rationale

AI platforms issue large numbers of workload credentials continuously. Without automated inventory and reconciliation, completed jobs leave behind tokens, secrets, and leases that remain active after their owning workload no longer exists. This control isolates inventory and orphan detection so teams can independently verify that credential state is reconciled against workload state.

## Scope

Applies to all credentials issued to AI and ML workloads including Vault dynamic secrets, cloud IAM temporary credentials, Kubernetes service account tokens, API keys for external services, and model registry authentication tokens. Covers inventory automation and orphaned credential detection tied to workload state.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Credential inventory system with automated reconciliation against workload state.
- Implementation Surface: Vault audit logs and lease inventory, cloud IAM credential reports, Kubernetes service account token tracking, pipeline orchestrator job state correlation, and orphaned credential remediation workflows.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; ML Engineering; Cloud Platform Engineering
