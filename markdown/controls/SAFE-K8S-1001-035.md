# SAFE-K8S-1001-035 - Centralized AI log retention lifecycle enforcement and durable retrieval

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must enforce retention periods aligned with organizational and regulatory requirements for each applicable centralized AI log class and must verify retained logs remain durably retrievable for audit and investigation.

## Objective

Ensure centralized AI workload logs are retained for required durations and remain durably retrievable when needed for audit, incident response, and regulatory record-keeping.

## Rationale

Tamper resistance alone is insufficient if centralized AI workload logs are deleted prematurely or cannot be retrieved when needed. Retention lifecycle enforcement and durable retrieval preserve the availability of centralized log evidence across required evidence windows.

## Scope

Applies to centralized logging backends and retained AI workload log classes, including audit logs, training and inference logs, GPU hardware logs, and platform security telemetry retained for operational or regulatory purposes. Covers retention policy configuration, lifecycle enforcement, and periodic retrieval validation. Excludes append-only immutability and cryptographic integrity verification mechanisms.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Retention lifecycle enforcement and durable retrieval validation.
- Implementation Surface: Retention policy configuration per log class, lifecycle enforcement on centralized log stores, durable retrieval checks, and periodic validation that retained logs remain available for audit and investigation.
- Owner Primary: Security Operations
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Infrastructure Engineering
