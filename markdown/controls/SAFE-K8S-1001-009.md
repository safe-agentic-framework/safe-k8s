# SAFE-K8S-1001-009 - Regulatory log and artifact retention policy enforcement

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.1 - Logging and Audit
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Organizations subject to regulatory requirements (EU AI Act, SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS) must enforce log and artifact retention policies that meet or exceed the minimum retention periods mandated by applicable regulations. Retention policies must cover API server audit logs, AI model training logs, model versioning artifacts, data provenance records, and human oversight decision logs. Automated enforcement must prevent premature deletion and ensure retention compliance is continuously verifiable.

## Objective

Ensure that all regulatory-required logs and AI artifacts are retained for the mandated periods and that retention compliance is automatically enforced and auditable.

## Rationale

Regulatory frameworks impose specific retention requirements for logs and artifacts related to AI system operation. The EU AI Act requires retention of logs generated by high-risk AI systems for periods appropriate to their intended purpose (Article 12, Article 72). Premature deletion of audit logs, training records, or model artifacts can result in regulatory non-compliance, inability to investigate incidents, and loss of evidence needed for post-market monitoring. Automated retention enforcement prevents human error and ensures continuous compliance.

## Scope

Applies to all Kubernetes clusters hosting AI workloads subject to regulatory retention requirements. Covers API server audit logs, AI training and inference logs, model version artifacts, data provenance records, and human oversight decision logs. Retention periods are defined by organizational policy based on applicable regulatory requirements.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Log management system retention configuration and artifact storage lifecycle policies.
- Implementation Surface: Log aggregation system retention policies (Elasticsearch ILM, Loki retention rules), object storage lifecycle policies for model artifacts (S3 lifecycle rules, GCS lifecycle management), immutable storage or WORM compliance for regulatory-critical logs, automated retention compliance scanning and alerting, and retention policy documentation mapping regulatory requirements to configured retention periods.
- Owner Primary: GRC/Compliance
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
