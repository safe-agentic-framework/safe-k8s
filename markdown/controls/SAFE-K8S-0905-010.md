# SAFE-K8S-0905-010 - Model promotion approval and reviewer identity validation

- Domain: D09 - AI Workload Security: Training, Serving, and Pipelines
- Knowledge Area: 9.5 - AI Supply Chain and Model Lifecycle
- Maturity: Advanced
- Classification: ai-specific
- Superseded By: SAFE-K8S-0905-024

## Statement

Model promotion approval workflows must validate reviewer identity through authenticated approval mechanisms, must enforce separation of duties between model developers and promotion approvers, and must record reviewer identity in the promotion audit trail.

## Objective

Ensure that model promotion approvals are attributable to authenticated, authorized reviewers with appropriate separation of duties.

## Rationale

Without reviewer identity validation, promotion approvals can be forged or attributed to unauthorized personnel. Without separation of duties, a single individual can both develop and approve a model for production, bypassing the intent of the review gate.

## Scope

Applies to all model promotion workflows requiring human approval on Kubernetes. Covers reviewer authentication, identity recording, and separation of duties enforcement. Excludes automated gate checks (SAFE-K8S-0905-005) and deployment infrastructure (SAFE-K8S-0905-011).

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Deployment approval workflow settings and identity provider integration for reviewer authentication.
- Implementation Surface: Promotion approval workflows with reviewer identity recording, identity provider integration (SSO, OIDC) for approval authentication, separation of duties policies preventing self-approval, and audit trail recording reviewer identity, timestamp, and approval decision.
- Owner Primary: Governance
- Owner Supporting: ML Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
