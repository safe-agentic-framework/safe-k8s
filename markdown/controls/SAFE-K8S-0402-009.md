# SAFE-K8S-0402-009 - Ephemeral training job credential lifecycle management

- Domain: D04 - Identity, Access, and Secrets Management
- Knowledge Area: 4.2 - Service Accounts and Workload Identity
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: ai-specific

## Statement

Credentials issued to ephemeral training jobs must be short-lived with expiration aligned to expected job duration, must be automatically rotated for long-running jobs, and must be revoked or cleaned up upon job completion or failure.

## Objective

Ensure that credentials for ephemeral training jobs do not outlive the workloads they serve, preventing credential reuse after job termination and reducing the window of exposure for compromised training infrastructure.

## Rationale

Training jobs are typically ephemeral but may run for hours or days and require access to sensitive resources including training datasets, model registries, and GPU clusters. If credentials issued to these jobs persist after completion, they become orphaned secrets that can be exploited. Long-running training jobs also need credential rotation to maintain access without using excessively long-lived tokens. Without automated cleanup, credential accumulation across thousands of training runs creates significant exposure.

## Scope

Applies to all ephemeral AI/ML training jobs including Kubernetes Jobs, Kubeflow TrainingJobs, Argo Workflow steps, and Spark training tasks. Covers both Kubernetes service account tokens and external credentials (cloud IAM, Vault tokens, database credentials) used by training workloads.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: Job controller configurations for credential TTL, projected token expiration settings, Vault dynamic secret TTLs, and post-job cleanup hooks or finalizers that revoke credentials on job completion.
- Implementation Surface: Projected ServiceAccountToken expirationSeconds aligned to job duration, Vault dynamic secret TTLs (1-4 hour leases), cloud workload identity federation short-lived credentials, Kubernetes Job finalizers and post-completion cleanup hooks for credential revocation, Kubeflow TrainingJob and Argo Workflow credential lifecycle configurations, and orphaned credential scanning for completed training runs.
- Owner Primary: ML Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; Security Engineering
