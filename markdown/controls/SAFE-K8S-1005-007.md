# SAFE-K8S-1005-007 - Vulnerability disclosure and coordination for Kubernetes infrastructure

- Domain: D10 - Observability, Incident Response, and Governance
- Knowledge Area: 10.5 - Incident Response for Kubernetes
- Maturity: Practitioner
- Classification: baseline

## Statement

Organizations must establish a vulnerability disclosure policy for their Kubernetes platform and AI infrastructure, define roles and responsibilities for receiving and triaging vulnerability reports, and implement coordination processes for vulnerabilities affecting shared infrastructure components. The policy must specify intake channels for internal and external reporters, triage SLAs, escalation paths for critical infrastructure vulnerabilities, and coordination procedures with upstream open-source projects and cloud providers when vulnerabilities span organizational boundaries.

## Objective

Ensure that vulnerabilities discovered in the Kubernetes platform and AI infrastructure are received, triaged, and remediated through a defined process with clear ownership, rather than handled ad hoc.

## Rationale

Kubernetes platforms consume many open-source components (CNI plugins, operators, ML frameworks) where vulnerabilities may be reported externally or discovered internally. Without a disclosure policy and coordination process, vulnerability reports are lost or delayed. SSDF task RV.1.3 requires vulnerability disclosure and remediation policy with supporting roles and processes.

## Scope

Kubernetes control plane, node components, cluster add-ons, AI/ML operators, and GPU infrastructure vulnerabilities affecting the platform layer.

## Enforcement

- Primary Enforcement Point: governance
- Implementation Surface: Vulnerability disclosure policy document, security@ or PSIRT intake channel, triage and escalation runbooks, SLA definitions for severity-based response times, coordination playbooks for multi-party vulnerabilities, integration with upstream Kubernetes security response committee processes.
- Owner Primary: Security Engineering
- Owner Supporting: Platform Engineering; ML Platform Engineering; Site Reliability Engineering
