# NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5 5.2.0 - SC-30(2)

- Framework Code: NIST-800-53R5
- Requirement Title: Randomness

## Mapping Notes

Randomization of agent deployment characteristics prevents predictable attack patterns

5.2 - IPAM (randomized IP assignment); 9.8 - Differential privacy (output randomization for information protection)
