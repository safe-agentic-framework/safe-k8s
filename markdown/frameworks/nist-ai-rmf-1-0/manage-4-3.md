# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - MANAGE 4.3

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Responses to the AI risks deemed high priority - as identified through the MAP function - are documented and monitored, and the risk management plan is updated as needed.

## Mapping Notes

IR playbooks document high-priority incident responses. Audit readiness dashboards monitor compliance posture. Threat intelligence integration ensures awareness of evolving high-priority threats.
