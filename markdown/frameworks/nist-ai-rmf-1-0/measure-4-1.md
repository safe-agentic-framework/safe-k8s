# NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 - MEASURE 4.1

- Framework Code: NIST-AI-RMF
- Requirement Title: Measurement approaches for identifying AI risks are connected to deployment context(s) and informed through consultation with domain experts and end users. Measurement results are documented.

## Mapping Notes

AI workload telemetry measures risks in production deployment context. Automated audit readiness generates documented compliance evidence tied to deployment configurations.
