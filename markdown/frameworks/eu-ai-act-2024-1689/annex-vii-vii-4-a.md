# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Annex-VII:VII(4)(a)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Conformity Based on Assessment of Quality Management System and Assessment of Technical Documentation

## Mapping Notes

The notified body shall assess the quality management system to determine whether it complies with the requirements of Article 17.

SAFE-K8S provides verifiable evidence for QMS technical components: CI/CD pipeline enforcement, automated security gates, and configuration management practices. The notified body assessment itself is external.
