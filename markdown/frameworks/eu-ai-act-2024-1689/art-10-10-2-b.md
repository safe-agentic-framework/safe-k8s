# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-10:10(2)(b)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Data and Data Governance

## Mapping Notes

Data governance practices shall concern data collection processes and the origin of data, and in the case of personal data, the original purpose of the data collection.

SAFE-K8S training data provenance controls (SAFE-K8S-0906-001) and ML-BOM generation (SAFE-K8S-0604-002) capture data origin and collection metadata at the infrastructure level. The legal assessment of original purpose is out of scope.
