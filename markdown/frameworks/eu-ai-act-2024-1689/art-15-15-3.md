# EU AI Act 2024/1689 - Art-15:15(3)

- Framework Code: EU-AI-ACT
- Requirement Title: Accuracy, Robustness and Cybersecurity

## Mapping Notes

The levels of accuracy and the relevant accuracy metrics of high-risk AI systems shall be declared in the accompanying instructions of use.

SAFE-K8S ML-BOM controls (SAFE-K8S-0604-002) capture accuracy metrics as infrastructure metadata, providing the data source for this declaration. The obligation to include them in instructions of use is an organizational concern, but SAFE-K8S provides the technical infrastructure to store and retrieve accuracy metrics.
